FailureModec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://IBCNServices.github.io/Folio-Ontology/Folio.owl#FailureMode

The specific manner or way by which a failure occurs in terms of failure of the item (being a part or (sub) system) function under investigation; it may generally describe the way the failure occurs. It shall at least clearly describe a (end) failure state of the item (or function in case of a Functional FMEA) under consideration. It is the result of the failure mechanism (cause of the failure mode)..
Is defined by
http://IBCNServices.github.io/Folio-Ontology/Folio.owl
Example
 Too high values.
